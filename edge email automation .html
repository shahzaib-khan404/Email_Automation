    <h2>Project Overview</h2>

  </div>


  <h3>Purpose</h3>

  <p>Edge Edu receives a continuous volume of client support emails related to their online certification program. The goal of this project is to build a fully automated Gmail monitoring system that uses Claude AI (Anthropic) to generate formal, accurate, FAQ-based email responses — with a human approval step before any email is sent.</p>


  <div class="callout purple">

    <span class="callout-icon">:dart:</span>

    <div class="callout-content">

      <strong>Primary Objective</strong>

      <p>Reduce manual email response time while maintaining response quality and accuracy by grounding every reply in the official Edge Edu FAQ knowledge base.</p>

    </div>

  </div>


  <h3>Goals</h3>

  <table>

    <thead>

      <tr><th>Goal</th><th>Description</th><th>Priority</th></tr>

    </thead>

    <tbody>

      <tr><td>Automated monitoring</td><td>Watch Gmail inbox and detect new client emails in real time</td><td><span class="badge must">Must Have</span></td></tr>

      <tr><td>AI-generated responses</td><td>Use Claude AI + FAQ content to draft accurate replies</td><td><span class="badge must">Must Have</span></td></tr>

      <tr><td>Human approval gate</td><td>Show draft to operator — send only after explicit approval</td><td><span class="badge must">Must Have</span></td></tr>

      <tr><td>Edit before send</td><td>Allow operator to modify AI draft before sending</td><td><span class="badge must">Must Have</span></td></tr>

      <tr><td>Formal corporate tone</td><td>All replies must use formal, professional language</td><td><span class="badge must">Must Have</span></td></tr>

      <tr><td>Fallback for unknown Qs</td><td>If FAQ doesn't cover the topic, AI answers generally or escalates</td><td><span class="badge should">Should Have</span></td></tr>

      <tr><td>Duplicate prevention</td><td>Never process the same email twice</td><td><span class="badge must">Must Have</span></td></tr>

    </tbody>

  </table>


  <h3>Out of Scope (v1.0)</h3>

  <p>The following are explicitly excluded from this version: web-based UI dashboard, multi-inbox support, auto-send without approval, CRM integration, analytics/reporting dashboard, and mobile app.</p>
